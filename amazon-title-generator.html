The Anatomy of a Perfect Amazon Title

1

Brand Name

If buyers search by brand. Skip if you're unknown — use keyword space instead.
2

Primary Keyword

The exact phrase with the highest search volume. This is non-negotiable — it must be here.
3

Top Feature

Your single biggest differentiator. Double-wall insulated. Wireless. Noise cancelling.
4

Second Feature / Spec

Capacity, size, model number, compatibility — whatever buyers filter by.
5

Audience / Use Case

"For Women", "Kids Age 3–8", "Home & Office" — only if it narrows intent helpfully.

Common Amazon Title Mistakes

🚫

Too Short

Titles under 80 characters waste ranking potential. Amazon gives you 200 — use at least 150.

🚫

Keyword Stuffing

"Best Water Bottle Water Bottle Insulated Water Bottle" looks spammy and Amazon will suppress it.

🚫

Promotional Language

Words like "Sale", "Free Shipping", "Best Price" violate Amazon's title policy and get listings suppressed.

🚫

All Caps

Fully capitalised titles are against Amazon's style guide. Use title case (First Letter Of Each Word).
