Etsy Tag Rules — What to Know

  • 📏Each tag can be up to 20 characters including spaces. Multi-word phrases count as one tag.
  • 🔢You get exactly 13 tag slots — use all of them, every time, on every listing.
  • 🔡Tags are not case sensitive — "Birthday Gift" and "birthday gift" are identical to Etsy's algorithm.
  • 🔄Repeat title keywords in tags — Etsy combines title and tags to determine relevance. Repetition reinforces the signal.
  • 🚫Don't use single broad words like "gift" or "handmade" alone — they're too competitive. Use phrases: "handmade leather gift".
  • ✅Include plurals separately if they change meaning — "keyring" and "keyrings" can both be valid tags.
